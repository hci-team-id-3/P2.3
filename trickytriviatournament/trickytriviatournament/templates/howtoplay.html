Welcome to The Tricky Trivia Tournament!

Background Information

This trivia game has a twist compared to your typical trivia game. The game will test the players knowledge in the following 5 categories:

  1. Texas Tech University
  2. Computer Science
  3. Food
  4. Sports
  5. Anime/Manga

Each game round is 10 questions, consisting of 2 from each category.

Instructions

When playing for the first time, user must connect to server first then log-in using admin credentials. After doing so, they must create their own customized log-in credentials.

  1. Player log-in
  2. Navigate main-menu
  3. Learn how to play the game by clicking on 'How to Play'
  4. Read through instructions then click 'Back to Menu'
  5. Track high scores on the 'Leaderboard' to see where you stand
  6. When you're done, click 'Back to Menu'
  7. Start a new game
  8. Questions will appear 1 by 1, play must answer to move to next question
  9. Select an answer
  10. Feedback is given on screen both visually and via sound effects before moving on
  11. Go through all 10 questions in the game round
  12. At the end of the round, your score will be displayed on screen
  13. Click 'Play Again' or 'Back to Menu'

Congratulations, you are now ready to play the game! Goodluck!
